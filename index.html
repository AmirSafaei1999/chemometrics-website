Chemometrics · Chromatography · Metabolomics

Research Focus

Advanced Chemometrics

Multivariate curve resolution, pattern recognition, and design of experiments for complex chemical data.

Chromatography & Hyphenated Techniques

GC-MS, LC-MS, and GC-IMS data processing for untargeted metabolomics and environmental analysis.

Food Authenticity

Machine learning and volatilomics to verify origin, quality, and adulteration of food products.

Machine Learning in Analytical Chemistry

AI-assisted method development, spectral deconvolution, and predictive modelling.
